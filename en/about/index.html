About

An indie developer focused on Apple platforms.

Hi, I’m liyb. I love building small, refined utilities for macOS and iOS — rather than piling on features, I care about whether every detail feels just right: whether the animation is smooth, whether it follows the system’s design language, whether it genuinely saves you time.

My products all revolve around a few principles:

  • Native first — crafted separately for macOS and iOS, never a cross-platform shell.
  • Privacy first — your data stays in your hands: nothing collected, uploaded, or tracked.
  • Always polishing — every app is a long-term iteration, not a one-off release.

If you enjoy these tools, or have any ideas and suggestions, feel free to get in touch.
