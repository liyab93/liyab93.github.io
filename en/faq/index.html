FAQ

The most common answers about pricing, updates and privacy.

Are the apps paid?

Some apps are free to download; others are a one-time purchase or unlock features via in-app purchase. See each app page and the App Store for exact pricing.

How do I get updates?

Versions downloaded from the App Store update automatically; you can also check for a new version inside the app.

Is my data safe?

Every app follows a privacy-first principle: your data stays on your device by default, is never uploaded to any server, and contains no third-party tracking. See each app’s privacy policy for details.

Which OS versions are supported?

Each app’s requirements are shown on its detail page (fetched live from the App Store). In general a recent macOS / iOS version is recommended for the best experience.

What if I run into a problem?

The fastest way is to email me from the support page — I read and reply to every message.
